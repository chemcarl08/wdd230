Chamber Site Plan

Site Name

The site name selected is Saskatoon Chamber of Commerce. This name represents a city in which the chamber operates. The name was selected because it reflects the locale and purpose of the chamber, which is to serve the Saskatoon community by promoting local businesses and fostering economic development.

Site Purpose

The purpose of the Saskatoon Chamber of Commerce website is to act as a regional economic catalyst, leadership convener, and community champion. The site will:

  • Promote business through member services, economic and community development, and public policy that balances economic prosperity.
  • Provide a platform for business-to-business networking and community events.

Scenarios

  • What events will the chamber be holding this month that promote business-to-business networking?
  • Where can I find contact information for the chamber's board of directors?
  • What has been the population growth in the area?

Color Schema

The color schema for the site includes:

  • Dark Blue (#2c3e50): Used for the header, footer, and headings.
  • Green (#18bc9c): Used for accents and links.
  • Light Grey (#ecf0f1): Used for the background color.

Typography

The typography for the site includes:

  • Roboto: Used for body text.
  • Open Sans: Used for headings.
